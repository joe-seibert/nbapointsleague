Draft Pick Quality

Each cell shows a player's pick slot vs. how they're producing in the playoffs. The cell color and Δ value tell you how much better or worse the pick has played than its slot.

Δ
pick number minus drafted-pool rank. +12 = produced 12 spots better than drafted; −8 = 8 spots worse.
DR
Drafted rank — where this player ranks by playoff points among the 48 drafted players (1 = top scorer in the league).
OR
Overall rank — where this player ranks by playoff points among every playoff scorer (~230 players, including undrafted bench guys).
frozen
Player's NBA team is out of the playoffs — their points (and rank) are locked in.
